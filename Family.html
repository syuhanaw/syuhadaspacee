This is my father!

Rosli bin Omar

This is my dad. He passed away when I was 13. Growing up without him was kind of rough. My dad loves many things;
but the one thing that is his outmost favourite would be his
job. He made me understand that whenever we do
a task that we absolutely loves, it feels like the
energy was never drained from us. I was so fond of him; he was my best
friend, my enemy as well as
my caretaker. Oh, how I miss having him near. He
was a good father, but back then we don't know it yet. Now that we knew,
we no longer have the chance to tell him.
